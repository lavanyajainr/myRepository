Ad Blockers:Setback or spark for Innovation

In 2015, the industry lost a staggering $22 billion in revenue due to ad blockers. Publishers are lamenting their losses. But if users are annoyed enough at ads that they want to block them, shouldn’t advertisers be doing something different?

This rough downturn could be the spark the industry needs to disrupt the old paradigms and create innovative ads for a new digital landscape.
